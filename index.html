Check out a few of my works!
Fuel Station Payment

The payment process at fuel stations takes more time than actual refueling of the vehicle’s fuel tank. Hence, an alternative contact-less method is needed to both expedite the payment process and also to eliminate physical handling of cash.

Muscle & Strength

We have designed a mobile application and smart watch for the audiences who want to stay updated through the charts and routines of their workout. The app will help the people to lose weight, gain muscle, maintain fitness and also to achieve the body shape that they desire.

Ed-Vantage

In this project, I understood the existing examination system and thereafter tried to re-engineer the examination process in order to reduce the teacher’s and EDP operator’s work load by using technology to automate certain processes.

OnePlus Watch

October 2020, There were rumours in the market that OnePlus was planning to launch a new digital product, Smart Watch. I gathered it's information and visualised it's User Interface. I have tried to design the same in this project.

cloudPass

The purpose of this project is to eliminate the problems with multiple login credentials on different social media platforms, that a user faces because of multiple platforms having different patterns for these credentials.
